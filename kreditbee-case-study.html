Lever A — Our Focus

A user who never borrows a second loan from you has contributed minimally to CLV. Kreditbee's biggest leak is users who take a first loan, have a bad experience (unclear terms, confusing process, poor communication), and never return. Fixing first-loan experience is the fastest way to unlock repeat borrowing.

Lever B — Our Focus

Unit economics require repeat loans. A user who borrows once every 6 months and completes the loan will generate far more lifetime value than a user who borrows once and leaves. The problem: users don't trust Kreditbee enough to come back because they don't understand the platform.

The Real Problem: Users don't trust Kreditbee because the loan experience is opaque. They don't understand interest rates, EMI calculations, eligibility criteria, or why they were rejected. After a bad first experience, switching to Bajaj Finserv or DMI Finance costs nothing.

Causal Chain:

Root Cause
Loan application process lacks clarity and transparency

→ User Experience
User confused about terms, rates, eligibility. Rejection without explanation

→ Short-Term Signal
User abandons app, doesn't apply again

→ Retention Impact
First→Second loan conversion drops. User never becomes repeat borrower

→ Business Impact
CLV collapses. Acquisition spend wasted. Competitive defection.

Problem Breakdown

  • Clarity broken — First loan experience is confusing
    • Interest rates shown, but users don't understand effective cost
    • EMI calculation opaque. Users surprised at commitment size
    • Eligibility criteria not clear upfront. Application rejection feels arbitrary
  • Conversion broken — Users don't return
    • No reason given for rejection. User assumes disqualified forever
    • No visibility into when eligibility might improve
    • Trust not built. User has no habit loop. Easy to switch
  • Root causes of first-loan failure
    • Product assumes financial literacy users don't have
    • No education on rates, terms, commitment upfront
    • Rejection messaging is technical, not human
03 — Stakeholders

Who is Directly Affected?

🧑 User / Borrower

Wants a simple, transparent way to borrow money. Wants to understand what they're signing up for. A single bad experience (hidden fees, confusing process, unfair rejection) destroys trust that takes many good experiences to rebuild. Zero switching cost — Bajaj Finserv is one app download away.

Lever: Clear terms → builds trust → comes back for second loan → becomes repeat borrower

🏢 Kreditbee

Needs repeat borrowers for unit economics to work. Every churned first-time user is acquisition cost with near-zero lifetime value return. High support volume and confused users signal product friction that's killing growth.

Lever: Clarity in first experience → higher first→second conversion → better CLV → efficient growth

04 — Solution

What Product Should Build

The gap isn't financing capability. Kreditbee can lend. The gap is clarity and trust-building. Users need to understand the journey, terms, and their chances before they commit.

Solution 1: Pre-Application Transparency Dashboard

Before users apply, show them exactly what they'd qualify for and why. Surface this in plain language.

  1. User enters income and loan amount needed
  2. App shows: "You likely qualify for ₹50,000-₹100,000. Here's why."
  3. Break down interest rate: "6-month loan: ₹X in interest. 12-month loan: ₹Y in interest."
  4. Show EMI upfront: "If you borrow ₹50k, your monthly payment is ₹XXX."
  5. Explain criteria: "You meet income, employment, and credit requirements."

Solution 2: Rejection Clarity and Next Steps

When users are rejected, don't leave them guessing. Give them a roadmap to qualify.

  1. Surface reason for rejection clearly: "Your credit score is 580. We require 650+."
  2. Give actionable steps: "Pay off ₹X in outstanding debt to improve eligibility."
  3. Show timeline: "Check back in 30 days. Your score should improve by then."
  4. Allow retry: Enable users to reapply after improvements, with in-app reminders
  5. Build trust: Offer lower-amount loans ("Qualify for ₹10k loan now") as trust builders

Solution 3: Post-Loan Engagement Loop

After first successful loan, build habit and trust for second borrowing.

  1. Track repayment milestones: "You've paid 3 EMIs on time. Great!"
  2. Show eligibility growth: "Your credit score improved to 650. You now qualify for ₹150k."
  3. Proactive outreach: "Need funds? You qualify for ₹75k more at 5.5% rate."
  4. Gamify trust: "Loan streak: 12 on-time payments. You're eligible for better rates."
