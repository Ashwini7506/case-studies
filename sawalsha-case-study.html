Sawalsha Activation — PM Case Study

01 — Framework

What actually drives user activation?

Lever A (Application Completion Rate) × Lever B (First to Second Loan Conversion) = Active User Base

Payday loan platforms face a brutal activation problem. Users start applications but abandon due to KYC friction. Users who complete their first loan never return for a second. Without repeat borrowers, unit economics collapse.

Lever A — Application Completion

A user who abandons during KYC or approval never becomes revenue. Lengthy documentation, confusing steps, and slow approval kill completion rates.

Lever B — First to Second Loan

A user who borrows once and leaves is expensive to acquire but generates minimal lifetime value. The critical window is 30-60 days after first loan repayment.

02 — The Problem

Where activation is actually breaking

50-60% Users who abandon loan applications during KYC or documentation stage
2-3 days Approval time that causes users to seek faster competitors
30-40% First-time borrowers who never apply for second loan
30-60 days Critical window after first repayment to activate second loan

The problem isn't loan approval capability. It's that users abandon before approval and never return after first loan. KYC friction kills application completion. Lack of engagement kills repeat borrowing.

Root Cause
KYC requires too many documents. Approval takes 2-3 days. No communication during wait.
→
User Experience
User uploads Aadhaar, PAN, salary slip, waits. No status update. Competing apps approve in 30 minutes.
→
Short-Term Signal
50-60% users abandon application. Those who complete get loan but don't engage again.
→
Activation Impact
High CAC with low completion rate. First-time borrowers become one-time borrowers. LTV collapses.
→
Business Impact
Unsustainable unit economics. CAC payback requires 3-4 loans but users stop at 1.

Problem Breakdown

Activation collapses at two points

  • KYC friction: Users start application, hit document upload, get confused or frustrated, abandon. 50-60% drop-off.
  • Post-loan ghosting: Users repay first loan, receive no engagement, forget about platform. 30-40% never return.

Root causes of abandonment

  • KYC requires scanning/uploading multiple documents — users on urgent need don't have time
  • Approval takes 2-3 days with zero status updates — users assume rejection or seek faster alternatives
  • After first loan repayment, no proactive outreach — users forget platform exists
  • Competing platforms offer instant approval with DigiLocker KYC — Sawalsha feels slow
03 — Stakeholders

Who is directly affected?

Stakeholder 1 → Borrower (User)

Needs urgent cash (payday loan scenario). Every hour of delay means late bill payment or missed opportunity. Wants instant approval, minimal friction. If Sawalsha is slow, switches to MoneyTap or Paytm Postpaid.

↑ Faster approval, easier KYC → completes application → becomes active borrower

Stakeholder 2 → Sawalsha

Needs repeat borrowers for unit economics. High CAC only justified if users borrow 3-4 times. Every user who borrows once and leaves is a loss.

↑ Higher activation → repeat borrowing → sustainable economics

04 — Solution

What product should build

The gap isn't risk assessment capability. It's making KYC frictionless and keeping users engaged post-first-loan.

Solution 1 · DigiLocker Integration + Instant KYC

Reduce KYC friction by pulling documents directly from government DigiLocker.

  • Integrate DigiLocker API — users authenticate once, all documents auto-fetched (Aadhaar, PAN, address proof)
  • Show real-time progress: "Step 1 of 3: Verifying Aadhaar... Done. Step 2: Verifying PAN..."
  • Instant preliminary approval: "You're approved for ₹10,000. Final check in 30 minutes."
  • Fallback for users without DigiLocker: Manual upload with clear instructions and sample images
  • Track drop-off per KYC step: Identify where users abandon most, optimize that step first

Solution 2 · Post-Loan Engagement + Pre-Approval Notifications

Activate repeat borrowing by proactively engaging users after first loan repayment.

  • Track repayment milestone: "You've paid 2 of 3 EMIs on time. Great progress!"
  • Pre-approval after full repayment: "Congrats on repaying your loan! You're pre-approved for ₹15,000."
  • Show eligibility increase: "Your credit limit increased to ₹20,000 based on timely repayment."
  • 30-day post-repayment nudge: "Need funds? You're pre-approved. Apply now for instant approval."
  • Gamify trust: "Loan streak: 1 on-time repayment. Next tier unlocks better rates."

Solution 3 · Real-Time Application Status + Transparency

Reduce abandonment during approval wait by showing exactly what's happening.

  • Live status dashboard: "Your application is in risk review. Estimated completion: 2 hours."
  • Push notifications at each stage: "KYC verified. Moving to risk assessment."
  • Show comparison: "90% of users get approved within 4 hours. You're on track."
  • Fallback messaging if delayed: "High demand today. Your approval will complete by 6 PM."
  • Reduce uncertainty: Users who know status stay engaged. Silence causes abandonment.
05 — Success Metrics

How we know it's working

Application completion rate is the fastest feedback signal. First to second loan conversion is the slower but truer measure of whether activation is working.

Key Metrics to Track

  • Application Completion Rate: % of started applications that reach approval stage (target: 70-80%)
  • KYC Drop-Off Rate: % of users abandoning at document upload (target: <20%)
  • Approval Time (Median): Time from application submit to approval decision (target: <4 hours)
  • First to Second Loan Conversion: % of first-time borrowers who apply for second loan within 90 days (target: 50-60%)
  • LTV / CAC Ratio: Lifetime value per user vs acquisition cost (target: 3:1 or better)
