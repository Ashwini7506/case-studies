Sawalsha Activation

01 — Framework

What drives user activation?

Core Equation Application Completion Rate × First-to-Second Loan Conversion = Active User Base

Breaking Down the Metrics

Application Completion Rate

Depends on:

  • Sign-up rate: Users who start application vs visitors
  • KYC completion rate: Users who successfully upload/verify documents
  • Form submission rate: Users who complete personal/employment details
  • Approval wait tolerance: Users who don't abandon during 2-3 day approval

First-to-Second Loan Conversion

Depends on:

  • On-time repayment rate: Users who complete first loan successfully
  • Post-loan engagement: Users who receive/respond to re-engagement outreach
  • Pre-approval awareness: Users who know they qualify for second loan
  • Repeat need timing: Users who need funds again within 30-90 days
02 — The Problem

Where activation breaks

50-60% Users abandon during KYC/document upload stage
2-3 days Approval wait time causing competitive switching
30-40% First-time borrowers who never apply for second loan
30-60 days Critical re-engagement window after first repayment
💡 Key Insight: Users don't abandon because they don't need loans. They abandon because the process is too slow and competitors offer instant approval. After first loan, they forget the platform exists.
03 — Who Are the Users?

Understanding borrower needs

👤 Persona 1: Ravi, Small Shop Owner (Age 35)
Runs a small kirana store. Needs ₹10,000 to restock inventory before festival season. Has smartphone but limited tech literacy. Uses WhatsApp daily.
Pain Point: Can't figure out how to scan and upload PAN card properly. Multiple failed attempts. Gives up and asks neighbor for cash loan instead.
👤 Persona 2: Priya, College Student (Age 21)
Needs ₹5,000 for laptop repair urgently. Tech-savvy but first-time borrower. Worried about hidden fees and interest rates. Compares multiple apps.
Pain Point: Applies to Sawalsha, waits 3 days with no status update. Gets instant approval from MoneyTap in 30 minutes. Never returns to Sawalsha.
👤 Persona 3: Amit, Delivery Partner (Age 28)
Daily wage earner. Borrowed ₹8,000 once, repaid on time. Would borrow again but forgot about Sawalsha. Uses phone mostly for navigation and calls.
Pain Point: Needs funds again after 2 months but doesn't remember Sawalsha exists. No reminder, no re-engagement. Discovers new competitor through ad.
04 — Solutions Tied to Users

What product should build

Solution 1 · One-Tap DigiLocker KYC

For Ravi (Shop Owner): No manual document upload. DigiLocker fetches Aadhaar/PAN automatically. Simple "Approve Access" button. Done in 30 seconds.

  • Integrate DigiLocker API for instant document verification
  • Show progress: "Verifying Aadhaar... ✓ Done. Verifying PAN... ✓ Done."
  • Fallback: Video KYC for users without DigiLocker (live agent assists)
  • Impact: KYC completion rate increases from 40% to 75%

Solution 2 · Real-Time Approval Status + Instant Pre-Approval

For Priya (Student): Show live status. "Your application is in risk review (2 of 3 steps). Estimated approval: 2 hours." Push notification when approved.

  • Live dashboard showing application progress in real-time
  • Push notifications at each stage: KYC verified → Risk assessment → Approved
  • Instant pre-approval for low-risk users: "You qualify for ₹10,000. Confirm to disburse."
  • Impact: Approval wait abandonment drops from 30% to 10%

Solution 3 · Post-Loan Engagement + WhatsApp Reminders

For Amit (Delivery Partner): After first loan repayment, send WhatsApp: "Congrats! You're pre-approved for ₹12,000. Need funds? Apply now."

  • Track repayment milestones: "2 of 3 EMIs paid. You're doing great!"
  • 30 days post-repayment: WhatsApp notification with pre-approval amount
  • Show eligibility increase: "Your limit increased to ₹15,000 based on good repayment."
  • Gamify: "Loan streak: 1. Unlock better rates with 3 on-time loans."
  • Impact: First-to-second loan conversion increases from 30% to 55%

Track These Metrics

  • KYC Completion Rate: Target 75%+ (from 40% baseline)
  • Application-to-Approval Time (Median): Target <4 hours (from 2-3 days)
  • Approval Wait Abandonment: Target <10% (from 30%)
  • First-to-Second Loan Conversion (90 days): Target 55%+ (from 30%)
  • LTV/CAC Ratio: Target 3:1 or better (sustainable unit economics)
Bottom Line Activation isn't about better algorithms. It's about understanding that Ravi can't scan documents, Priya won't wait 3 days, and Amit forgets you exist. Fix those human problems, activation follows.
